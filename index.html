Internet Routers

Routers are WiFi signals, connecting wireless devices to the World Wide Web


Description of a Router

Routers are like a connection between two groups. They serve as a transition from the Web to your device, and can transmit information, signals, and other data. Routers have security though passwords and other features, and this minimizes the risk of getting your information stolden by hackers or threats.

What are some types of routers?

Wired Router: Wires connect the device with the router. The device has a port where the cable is connected. The router has cables that run to other computers or other devices to transmit information to the web and back.

Wireless Router: Antennas transmit information from the router to the modem and back to your device. The router is connected to a modem, which can the transmit information to the Web and back. The router acts as a switch from wired communication to an easy connection using antennas.
